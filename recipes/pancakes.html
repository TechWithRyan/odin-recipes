Pancakes

Description

Try this great tasting lasagna recipe. You will love it!

Ingredients

  • Pasta slice
  • Minced beef
  • Creamer
  • Salt & spices
  • Garlic & Onion
  • Cooking oil
  • Pure tomatoes
  • Pepper

Follow these steps

  1. Heat the oven to 175 degrees
  2. Warm up the pan
  3. Add cooking oil & garlic
  4. Add well cut onions
  5. Add the minced beef
  6. Place pasta slices and build the lasagna then place it in the oven for 40 minutes. Then add cheese and sauce.
